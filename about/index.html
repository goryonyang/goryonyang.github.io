I am a Ph.D. student in Cyberspace Security at Guangzhou University, advised by Prof. Zhiming Zheng and Prof. Jin Li. My research interests include blockchain, secure distributed algorithms, and applied cryptography.

I received the Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering from Ludong University in 2019, and the Master's degree in Computer Science from Qufu Normal University in 2021.
