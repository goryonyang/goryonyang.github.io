Biography
Guoyu Yang is now a Ph.D. student at the Institute of Artificial Intelligence and Blockchain, Guangzhou University. He received the B.S. degree from Ludong University in 2019 and the M.S. from Qufu Normal University in 2021, respectively. His research interests are blockchain, distributed computing, and applied cryptography.
